Tools of the Raid

Well known and part of the image of a Viking warrior. These weapons came in many shapes and sizes.

Many warriors of the north perfered the sword over the now iconic axe.

One of the earliest weapons known to mankind, the spear was put to great effect within a shield wall.

Not as well regarded or associated with the Vikings but the shield was the bread and butter of the infamous shield wall that allow Viking warriors to win against numerically superior forces.
